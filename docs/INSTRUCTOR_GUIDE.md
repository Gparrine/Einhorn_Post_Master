# Einhorn Postmaster — Instructor Guide

**App link:** https://gparrine.github.io/Einhorn_Post_Master/

Einhorn Postmaster is a single place to write one class or event announcement and send it to the club’s main channels: **Discord**, **Facebook**, **Meetup**, and **Gymdesk**. Instead of retyping the same details four times, you compose once and publish from here.

---

## Before you start

Every post should include:

- **A date** — month and day is enough (e.g. `June 28` or `6/28`). The current year is assumed.
- **A list of activities** for the class or event (what you’ll be working on, any special focus, reminders, etc.)

You can write a **short draft** and click **Refine with AI** to expand it with the usual Einhorn details (locations, times, signup link, tone, and so on). Review the result before sending.

---

## Writing your post

1. Open the app and type in the text box.
2. Use the toolbar for **bold**, *italic*, lists, and alignment if needed.
3. Click the gold **🦄** button to add emojis (favorites include unicorn, swords, fencer, sparkles, and bear).
4. Optional: click **Refine with AI** to polish a minimal draft.
5. Read through and edit anything that needs changing.

---

## Sending

Click the button for the platform you want, or **Send to All** to go through each one in sequence. You’ll always get a confirmation dialog before anything is sent.

| Platform | What happens |
|---|---|
| **Discord** | Posts automatically to the club’s `#meetings-plans` channel. |
| **Facebook** | Text is copied and your Facebook group opens — **paste and click Post** there. |
| **Meetup** | Text is copied and your group’s events page opens — find the event for that date, **Edit**, paste into **Description**, and save. |
| **Gymdesk** | Text is copied and Gymdesk opens — go to **Gym → Schedule**, find the session for that date, **Edit Session → Description**, paste, and save. |

A **green check** on a button means that step succeeded. For Facebook, Meetup, and Gymdesk, you still need to paste and save on that site — the app prepares the text and opens the right page for you.

If something goes wrong, a **red X** appears with a short error message at the bottom of the screen.

---

## Tips

- **Include the date in the post** so Meetup and Gymdesk can match the right class.
- **Refine with AI** works best when you’ve already noted the date and main activities — it fills in the rest.
- **Send to All** posts to Discord automatically, copies your text once, then shows **Open Facebook**, **Open Meetup**, and **Open Gymdesk** buttons — click each link, paste, and save.

---

## Quick workflow example

1. Write: `June 28 — longsword fundamentals, partner drills, open sparring`
2. Click **Refine with AI** and review
3. Click **Send to All**
4. Confirm each send; paste on Facebook, Meetup, and Gymdesk when those tabs open
5. Discord goes live on its own

That’s it — one draft, four channels.
